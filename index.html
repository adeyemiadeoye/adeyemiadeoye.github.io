Adeyemi D. Adeoye

I am currently a Research Fellow in the Dynamical Systems, Control, and Optimization (DYSCO) research group, IMT Lucca, Italy on an ERC-Advanced Grant project, led by Prof. Alberto Bemporad.

I completed my PhD at IMT Lucca in June 2025, and was fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Alberto Bemporad. My thesis was based on the development and analysis of quasi-Newton methods for solving nonsmooth optimization problems in learning and control (Thesis, Slides). From August 2023 to January 2024, I visited Prof. Philipp Petersen’s Math ML group at the University of Vienna (Austria) as an Erasmus+ Trainee. Previously, I studied at AIMS twice (MSc) and the University of Ilorin (BSc).

  Curriculum Vitae

Research topics

Mathematical optimization for engineering applications: theoretical foundations, algorithmic development, and practical implementation in machine learning, control systems, and other interdisciplinary domains.
