I am currently a Research Fellow in the Dynamical Systems, Control, and Optimization (DYSCO) group at IMT Lucca (Italy). I work on an ERC Advanced Grant project led by Prof. Alberto Bemporad. My research focuses on mathematical optimization for engineering applications, spanning theoretical foundations, algorithm design, and practical implementations in machine intelligence, control systems, and interdisciplinary domains.
